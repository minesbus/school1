The educational proposal of TSOF is mainly focused on primary school on forming oneself as a person. You learn values such as responsibility, respect, honesty, justice, freedom, empathy, solidarity, perseverance, gratitude, tolerance, among others. In secondary school, the focus is on forming your identity—discovering your interests, your choices, the people you like to surround yourself with, your orientation, and everything you need to discover.

la propuesta educativa del TSOF es enfocada principalmente en primaria para formarse como persona, aprendes valores, responsabilidad,respeto,honestidad,justicia,libertad,empatia,solidaridad,perseverancia,gratitud,tolerancia,entre otros. y en secundaria a formarse como identidad, descubrir tus gustos, tus elecciones, con quien te gusta rodearte, tu orientacion, y todo lo que necesites descubrir.
